Arabic Small Web is a curated collection of Arabic-language personal blogs and websites. Inspired by the Kagi Small Web project, it aims to make it easier to discover independent Arabic voices on the web.

How it works

We collect RSS feeds from a curated list of personal blogs and websites. A GitHub Action runs daily to fetch new posts and regenerate this static site. No backend, no database, just simple HTML.

Add your site

If you have a personal blog or website in Arabic and would like to be included, please open an issue with your site name, URL, and RSS feed link.

Why?

The Arabic web is often dominated by large platforms. This project celebrates individual voices: people writing about their lives, ideas, and experiences in their own spaces.
